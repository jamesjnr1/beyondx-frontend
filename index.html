About the Programme

Work that restores dignity.
Hire that builds trust.

BeyondX is a structured work-release and reintegration programme operated in partnership with the Ghana Prisons Service. It places vetted, skill-matched workers from correctional facilities into verified short-term roles with responsible employers across Greater Accra.

Every worker completes skills certification and carries a digital work record tracked in real time. Every employer receives GPS-verified attendance logs and dedicated coordinator support.

Platform Design

Built for progression, transparency, and trust.

Rank Progression
Workers advance through Bronze, Silver, and Gold ranks. Higher ranks unlock higher-paying contracts. Every completed task and positive rating moves a worker forward.
In-App Payments
All payments are processed through the BeyondX platform. Every transaction is tracked. A 15% platform commission is deducted per transaction to sustain programme operations.
Offense Classification
Employer profiles show a clear offense classification. High-profile crimes carry a ⚠️ disclaimer. Lower offences are simply tagged as minor — full transparency without unnecessary stigma.

Before You Proceed
Important Notice Regarding Our Talent Pool

All individuals listed on this platform are verified participants in Ghana's national reintegration programme. Prior to listing, each candidate has undergone background screening conducted in partnership with the Ghana Prisons Service.

Regarding Criminal History

Candidates have served their sentences in full and are legally recognised as having fulfilled their debt to society. Criminal history is disclosed transparently on each profile. Where an individual has been convicted of a more serious offence, their profile will carry a clearly visible ⚠️ Major Offense Tag.

What This Means For You As An Employer

  • You are hiring a vetted, work-ready individual who has completed their legal sentence
  • All candidates have been assessed as fit for reintegration into the workforce
  • Profiles tagged ⚠️ are disclosed in the interest of full transparency, not automatic disqualification
  • Hiring through this platform may qualify your business for government tax incentives
  • All job completions are tracked and recorded through the platform

Your Role As An Employer

We ask that all employers engage every candidate with fairness and human dignity. By using this platform, you agree to evaluate candidates based on their skills, conduct, and verified work history — not on judgment alone.

Before You Begin
Welcome. You Belong Here.

This platform was built for you — not as a favour, but because your skills, your time, and your willingness to work have value.

Your Rights On This Platform

  • You have the right to fair pay for every completed job
  • You have the right to report any employer who treats you with disrespect or unfairness
  • No employer on this platform is permitted to harass, exploit, or demean you
  • More completed jobs means a stronger profile and access to better opportunities

One Thing We Ask Of You

Show up. Do the work. Treat every job — no matter how small — as a chance to prove what you are capable of. Your reputation here is yours to build, and no one can take it from you.
